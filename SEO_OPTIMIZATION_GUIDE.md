### B. Robots.txt
✅ **File Created**: `/robots.txt`
- Allows all crawlers for public pages
- Sets proper crawl delays to prevent server overload
- Points search engines to sitemap.xml
- Specific rules for Googlebot (faster crawling)

**Benefits**:
- Guides search engine crawlers efficiently
- Prevents crawling of private sections
- Optimizes crawl budget
